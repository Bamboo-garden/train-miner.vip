About Train-miner

Train-miner is an engaging online strategy game that combines railroad management with resource collection. In this captivating simulator, you'll take on the role of a railroad tycoon tasked with building and expanding your train network.

As you progress through the game, you'll need to collect various resources, optimize your routes, and upgrade your equipment to become the ultimate train mining magnate. The game features intuitive controls, attractive graphics, and progressively challenging levels to keep you engaged for hours.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a strategy enthusiast, Train-miner offers the perfect blend of resource management and railroad simulation. Test your skills and see how efficiently you can build your railroad empire!

How to Play

  1. Start by establishing your first mining station and connecting it to your central depot.
  2. Collect resources from various locations by extending your railway network.
  3. Manage your trains to ensure efficient transportation between mining sites and depots.
  4. Upgrade your infrastructure to increase capacity and efficiency.
  5. Overcome environmental challenges and optimize your routes for maximum productivity.
